Pygrunn Winter Hackaton

Let's get together and build something.

Rules

  • Any pythonista can join. It'd be nice to have a graphic designer join as well. We should advertise it on the Pygrunn mailing list and maybe other mailing lists in the Netherlands. If it gets too big we'll just do it in Haren :D
  • We get together and build (and finish!) something in a weekend.
  • The product must be online and usable at the end of the event.
  • The source code must be released as free software at the end of the event, so everyone who contributed can always show off what they built. I suggest we just make a public github/bitbucket repos.
  • Pitchers need to come up with an idea of a project that can be finished in one weekend. The requirements and a basic idea of how to organize the development must be clear from the start. The ideas need to be sent in advance, so that we can check that they are clear enough and help make it clearer if necessary.

Schedule

Saturday

  • All pitchers line up and get 5 minutes to explain their idea.
  • First voting.
  • The pitchers of the 3 best ideas get 15 minutes to explain their idea in more detail. 
  • Second voting.
  • Everyone gets to work. We can use scrum to track progress.
  • End of the day: First demo.

Sunday

  • Everyone continues work.
  • Afternoon: Final demo.
  • We give some publicity to what we built: Hackernews, Python mailing lists, Reddit. Heck, we could even issue a press release.
  • Beer!
